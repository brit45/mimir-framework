## When Do You Need a Commercial License?

You need a commercial license if you plan to use Mímir Framework in any of the following scenarios:

### ✅ Commercial License Required For:

- **Commercial Products**: Integration into products or services sold for profit
- **SaaS Applications**: Software-as-a-Service platforms
- **Industrial Applications**: Manufacturing, automation, production systems
- **Enterprise Deployments**: Internal use in for-profit organizations
- **Proprietary Software**: Closed-source applications
- **Cloud Services**: Hosted AI/ML services for customers
- **OEM Distribution**: Bundling with hardware or other software products
- **Consulting Services**: Using the framework for paid client projects
- **Any revenue-generating activity** using the software

### ❌ Commercial License NOT Required For:

- Personal projects and learning
- Academic research and education
- Non-profit organizations
- Open-source projects under GPL-2.0
- Internal tools for non-commercial purposes (hobbyist use)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I start with GPL-2.0 and switch to commercial later?
**A:** Yes! You can develop under GPL-2.0 and purchase a commercial license when ready to commercialize. However, you must ensure compliance with GPL-2.0 terms until the commercial license is active.

### Q: What happens if my commercial license expires?
**A:** Your software continues to work with the version you have. You lose access to updates, support, and the right to deploy new instances. You must either renew or remove the software from commercial use.

### Q: Can I modify the source code under a commercial license?
**A:** Yes, Professional and Enterprise licenses allow modifications for your internal use. Perpetual license includes redistribution rights.

### Q: Do I need a license for each deployment?
**A:** Startup license is limited to 3 deployments. Professional and Enterprise have unlimited deployments.

### Q: Is the commercial license transferable?
**A:** Licenses are non-transferable without written approval. Contact us if you need to transfer a license due to company acquisition or restructuring.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** Bank transfer, wire transfer, credit card (via invoice), and purchase orders for qualified organizations.
